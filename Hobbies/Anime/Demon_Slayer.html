Demon Slayer

Synopsis

A family is attacked by demons and only two members survive - Tanjiro and his sister Nezuko, who is turning into a demon slowly. Tanjiro sets out to become a demon slayer to avenge his family and cure his sister.

Favorite Character

Tanjirou Kamado

The kindest and most thoughtful, family-oriented protagonist.

I've never liked the main character most but Tanjiro is by far my most favorite.

He adores his family and does right by the, finds strength in their bonds and looks after those around him.

He finds a way to be kind, even to the enemy


Why did I enjoy this?

This anime has one of the best animations I have witnessed.

The story isn't all that unique but the soundtracks and the character dynamics keep me hooked.
