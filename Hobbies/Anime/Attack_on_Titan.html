Attack On Titan

Synopsis

After his hometown is destroyed and his mother is killed, young Eren Jaeger vows to cleanse the earth of the giant humanoid Titans that have brought humanity to the brink of extinction.

Favorite Character

Levi Ackerman

One of the most overpowered soldiers from the survey Corps

Levi is strong-willed and determined to ensure the survival of the Eldians

He does not shy away from a challenge and is resourceful.


Why did I enjoy this?

One of the best anime to release recently and has easily earned the top spot in my anime list

There's a lot of emotions that I went through and it was relatable in showing both sides being right in their own right when it comes to war
