Red Dead Redemption 2

Synopsis

After a robbery goes badly wrong in the western town of Blackwater, Arthur Morgan and the Van der Linde gang are forced to flee.

With federal agents and the best bounty hunters in the nation massing on their heels, the gang must rob, steal and fight their way across the rugged heartland of America in order to survive.

Favorite Character

Sadie Adler

She was an undisputed favorite. After the gang rescued her from the O'Driscolls who widowed her

She decided she would take matters into her own hands and refused to become a damsel.

Her level-headed and strong-willed personality really looped me in to her character.


Why did I enjoy this?

The open world element of this game is endless and keeps you occupied for hours on end.

There is so much to discover, so many different encounters and how they play out according to how it is approached.

The open world is riddled with unsolved mysteries and easter eggs of the real world.

Even to this day, some mysteries of this game are yet to be unraveled.
